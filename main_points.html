Moral Agent

A Moral Agent is a program that knows the difference between good and bad. It is a computer or robot that will be good in every sense we demand of the term; safe and not evil.

Trust

A Moral Agent must be software whose morality we trust. There must also be a mechanism that provides us with a guarantee that the program we think we are talking to, actually is who it claims to be.

Open

An Open Moral Agent is a program whose internal working is entirely known, predictable and understood. The moral principles guiding the computer/robot as well as what it would do under the most extreme, unusual or futuristic scenario must be open and accessible to everyone whose trust we are asking for.
